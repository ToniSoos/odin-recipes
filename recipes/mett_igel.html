Mettigel

Your friends having a party and as a guest with excellent guest you don't want to come with bare hands? I got you.

The Mettigel is the dish which can not be missed at a proper party.

It is easy to prepare and contains only three ingredients, minced pork onions and olives.

If it isn't clear by looking at the image what Mettigel means: Minced pork-hedgehog

Ingredients

  • Minced pork (a decent Mettigel should atleast have 1 kg)
  • Onions(amount depends on the size of the Mettigel)
  • A couple olives

Step-by-step instruction

  1. Give the minced pork a nice round shape. Thats gonna be the hedgehog's torso.
  2. Slice the onions and stick them into the Mettigel to simulate the spikes.
  3. Last but not least: Use olives for the eyes and the nose. Be creative!
