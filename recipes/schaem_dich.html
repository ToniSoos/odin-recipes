Oooouh. One of my favorite snacks of all time! Easy to make and even easier to enjoy!

To be fair, this desert is not typical german, it is also well know in the USA as "Shame Sticks". But whatever I do enjoy them too much to not mention them.

Check out these guys enjoying it!

Ingredients

  • 500g Butter(enough for 3-4)
  • Sugar

Step-by-step instruction

  1. Split the butter in sizes, I recommend 3-4
  2. Roll them in sugar, every side has to be covered sufficiently
  3. Poke a stick into them, so you can hold them like an ice cream
  4. Enjoy!
